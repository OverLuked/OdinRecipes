Carbonara

A filipino carbonara is different from the traditional carbonara as it is richer and creamier than the traditional carbonara. The filipino carbonara is more similar to that of a pasta alfredo with bacon.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb. spaghetti
  • 1 pc Knorr beef cube
  • 7 strips bacon
  • 15 oz all-purpose cream
  • 6 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pc (Chopped) yellow onion
  • 2 cloves (minced) garlic
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 qts water

Steps

Step1

Boil water and add salt as it boils. Cook the pasta within and drain the water.

Step2

Cook bacon in medium heat.

Step3

Saute garlic til light brown, and add mushrooms using the lefover bacon fat (oil).

Step4

Add knorr cube and crush it and cook with garlic sauce for another 30 seconds

Step5

Pour all-purposecream into the pot, stir for 4 minutes. Add cheese and the half-cooked bacon into the port.

Step6

Arrange the pasta with the sauce and add cheese on top of the pasata.
