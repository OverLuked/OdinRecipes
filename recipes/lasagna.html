ODIN RECIPIES

Lasagna

A filipino carbonara is different from the traditional carbonara as it is richer and creamier than the traditional carbonara. The filipino carbonara is more similar to that of a pasta alfredo with bacon.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb. spaghetti
  • 1 pc Knorr beef cube
  • 7 strips bacon
  • 15 oz all-purpose cream
  • 6 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pc (Chopped) yellow onion
  • 2 cloves (minced) garlic
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 qts water
