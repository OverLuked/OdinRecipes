Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic recipe made with a meat sauce as its base with a layer of noodes and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lbs Ground beef chunk
  • 1/2 diced medium onion
  • 1/2 diced large bell pepper (Green, red, or yellow)
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 28 oz Tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves (minced) garlic
  • 3 oz Tomato paste
  • 14 oz Crushed tomato
  • 2 tbsp Fresh or Dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup Fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder/salt
  • 1 tbsp Red Wine
  • 1 tbsp or 1/4 cup Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

Step1

Make the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.

Step2

Boil the pasta and simmer it.

Step3

Layer the pasta and put a cupt of meat sauce on top. Followed by a layer of sauce and a layer of cheese. Repeat this step till the ingredients are used up.

Step4

Bake the lasagna till its bubbly.
