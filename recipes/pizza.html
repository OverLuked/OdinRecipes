Pizza

A filipino carbonara is different from the traditional carbonara as it is richer and creamier than the traditional carbonara. The filipino carbonara is more similar to that of a pasta alfredo with bacon.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cornmeal
  • Cheese
  • Sauce of choice
  • Topic of choice

Steps

Step1

Make the dough and kneed it.

Step2

Punch and shape the dough in a circular shape.

Step3

Add the sauce of your choice on top of the dough and put a layer of cheese above.

Step4

Put your toppings of choice on top of the cheese and bake the pizza at a very high temperature.
